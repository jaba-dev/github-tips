  • Strong Suction Cup: Prefer gel + suction combo for extra strength, resists heat and bumps.
  • Sturdy Arm and Joints: Thicker arms or some metal parts are much better.
  • One-Handed Phone Mounting: Push-button locks or simple clips are best.

Car Charger - Top Features

  • Fast Charging Support: Look for USB-C PD (Power Delivery) or Qualcomm QC 3.0/4.0.
  • At Least 2 Ports: USB-C fast charging + USB-A regular port is the best combo.
  • Reliable Brand with Safety Features: Protects your phone and battery life.
  • Compact and Low Profile: Should not stick out too much from the cigarette lighter slot.
  • Good Cable (if included): High-quality cable that supports fast charging and data transfer.
