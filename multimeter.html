Multimeter Buying Guide for Home Use

Essential Features

  • AC Voltage: At least 400V AC.
  • DC Voltage: At least 600V DC.
  • AC/DC Current: Useful for motors, fuses, etc.
  • Resistance Measurement: To check cables and devices.
  • Continuity Test: Must have an audible beep.
  • Capacitance Measurement: Optional, but helpful.
  • Frequency Measurement: Optional bonus feature.

Convenience

  • Backlight Display: Essential for dark places.
  • True RMS: Better accuracy for AC measurements.
  • Auto-Ranging: Easier and safer for casual users.

Safety & Build

  • Safety Rating: CAT III 600V or CAT IV 300V minimum.
  • Protective Casing: Preferably with a rubber cover.
  • Good Quality Probe Leads: Thick, flexible, safe tips.

Extra Features (Optional)

  • Temperature Measurement.
  • Hold Button to freeze readings.
  • Low Battery Indicator.
  • Fuse-protected current measurement.

Recommended Brands

  • Uni-T (Best price/quality balance)
  • Klein Tools (Very good for DIY/home)
  • Brymen (Higher-end quality)
  • Fluke (Top quality, expensive)
